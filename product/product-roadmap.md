# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Sales CRM
Lead management, follow-ups, meetings, quotation generation, and services catalog.

### 2. Accounts
Payment confirmation, GST-compliant invoicing, lead-to-customer conversion, and refund workflows.

### 3. Customers
Customer records, detail views, services availed, documents, and payment history.

### 4. Case Management
Case creation, follow-up timelines, lawyer assignment, legal templates, automated document generation, and progress tracking.

### 5. Partners
Wealth Manager profiles, commissions, wallet, sub-partners, and performance tracking.

### 6. Team Management
Internal users and roles, lawyers directory, and HRMS for employee management.

### 7. Reports & Analytics
Sales, cases, accounts, and Wealth Manager performance dashboards with export.

### 8. Dashboard Home
Admin landing page with KPI widgets, activity feed, SLA alerts, and quick actions.
